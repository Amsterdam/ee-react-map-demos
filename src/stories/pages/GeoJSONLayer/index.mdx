### Usage scenarios

- **Visualizing Spatial Data:**

Points of Interest (POI): Display locations such as restaurants, tourist spots, and public services on a map.
Geospatial Analysis: Show results of geospatial analyses, such as cluster analysis or heat maps of density.

- **Thematic Mapping:**

Choropleth Maps: Color-coding regions (e.g., countries, states) based on data attributes like population density, income levels, or election results.
Environmental Data: Display regions affected by natural phenomena (e.g., flood zones, earthquake epicenters).

- **Interactive Maps:**

User Interaction: Allow users to click on features to get more information, such as popup windows showing data about the feature.
Dynamic Styling: Change the style of features based on user interactions or other dynamic inputs (e.g., highlight a region when hovered over).

- **Routing and Navigation:**

Path Visualization: Display routes for navigation purposes, such as walking paths, driving routes, or public transit lines.
Network Analysis: Show network connections, such as transportation networks or utility lines.

- **Administrative and Political Boundaries:**

Country/State Borders: Display administrative boundaries for countries, states, cities, or districts.
Electoral Maps: Show electoral boundaries and results, allowing users to explore voting patterns and results.

- **Event Mapping:**

Historical Events: Visualize the locations of historical events, battles, or natural disasters over time.
Live Data Feeds: Display real-time data such as the locations of current events, traffic conditions, or weather updates.

- **Resource Management:**

Natural Resources: Map the locations of resources like forests, water bodies, or mineral deposits.
Agricultural Data: Show areas of different crop types, soil quality, or land use patterns.


- **Urban Planning:**

Infrastructure Development: Display current and planned infrastructure projects such as new roads, public transit, or parks.
Zoning Maps: Show land use zoning and urban planning information.
